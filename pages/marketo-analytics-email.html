Metric definitions Hard Bounce — Permanent delivery failure (invalid/non-existent address, domain does not exist). Marketo suppresses the address automatically. High rates indicate list quality issues.
Soft Bounce — Temporary failure (mailbox full, server unavailable, message too large). Marketo retries automatically; persistent soft bounces are eventually treated as hard bounces.
Unsubscribe — Recipient opted out via the email's unsubscribe link. Marketo marks them as Marketing Suspended. Normal threshold: < 0.5% of delivered.
Complaint — Recipient clicked "Mark as spam" in their mail client (reported via FBL). Even 1–2 complaints signals a deliverability risk; high complaint rates can damage sender reputation and trigger ISP filtering.
